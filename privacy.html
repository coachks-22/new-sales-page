Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

VeloForge is an AI-powered cycling coaching platform that integrates with Garmin, WHOOP, Strava, and other services to provide personalized training guidance.

Information We Collect

When you connect your accounts, we receive training and health-related data from Garmin Connect and other platforms via their official APIs. This may include power metrics, heart rate, speed, distance, sleep data, HRV, readiness scores, and nutrition information. We do not store or ask for your login credentials.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data you share to deliver our core features: generating adaptive training plans (ForgeBlocks™ and RacePhase™), monitoring fatigue (Load Index™), adjusting daily training (Recovery Intelligence™ and Adaptive Volume Engine™), providing nutrition guidance (FuelSync™), and other AI coaching tools. Data is used only for these purposes.

Third-Party Integrations

VeloForge relies on official APIs from Garmin, Strava, WHOOP, and TrainingPeaks. We only access data you explicitly authorize us to receive. Once received, we are responsible for how we handle it in accordance with this policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized or aggregated data to improve our AI models, but identifiable data is not shared with third parties except as needed to provide the service or as required by law.

Your Rights

You can disconnect any integration at any time through your account settings. You may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting us at ks@veloforge.app. We will respond to valid requests as required by applicable law.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

For more details on how Garmin and Strava handle your data, please review their policies:

  • Garmin Privacy Policy
  • Strava Privacy Policy

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ks@veloforge.app.
