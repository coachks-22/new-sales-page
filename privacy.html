Privacy Policy

VeloForge is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights — including specific disclosures for our Garmin Connect IQ integration.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Activity data from your Garmin device: power output, heart rate, cadence, speed, distance, and elapsed time
  • Heart rate variability (HRV) where available from your device
  • Sleep data where available via Garmin device sync
  • Device model and firmware version (for compatibility only)
  • Basic account information: email address and subscription status
  • We do not permanently store GPS or precise location data beyond what is required to calculate ride metrics such as elevation and distance

2. How We Use Your Data

Data collected from your Garmin device and account is used exclusively to power the VeloForge coaching system:

  • Calculate your Load Index™ and daily training stress
  • Generate ForgeBlocks™ phase recommendations aligned to your race timeline
  • Power Recovery Intelligence™ daily adjustments based on HRV, sleep, and readiness
  • Calibrate ZoneLock™ interval targets using your power curve
  • Generate FuelSync™ fueling guidance matched to your workload

Your data is never used for advertising, audience profiling, or resale.

Garmin Connect IQ Data Statement

VeloForge accesses Garmin Connect IQ data only with the user's explicit authorization. Data obtained through Garmin Connect IQ is used exclusively to provide the VeloForge coaching service and is not transferred to unauthorized third parties.

For Garmin's own privacy practices, please review the Garmin Connect Privacy Policy →

3. Data Sharing

  • VeloForge does not sell your data to any third party — ever
  • Your data is not shared with advertisers or data brokers
  • Infrastructure providers (hosting, database) may process data strictly under data processing agreements that prohibit secondary use

4. Cookies

  • We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session
  • We may use analytics cookies to understand how the site is used (no personal data tied to these)
  • We do not use advertising or tracking cookies
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: your data is retained for the duration of your subscription
  • Cancelled accounts: data deleted within 30 days of a deletion request
  • You may request immediate deletion at any time by contacting us

6. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Delete — request permanent deletion of your data
  • Export — receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt out — withdraw consent for any non-essential processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at coachks@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at coachks@gmail.com
