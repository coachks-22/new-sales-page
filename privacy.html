Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

VeloForge ("we", "us", or "our") provides an AI-powered cycling coaching platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information, particularly when you connect third-party services like Garmin, WHOOP, Strava, and TrainingPeaks.

Information We Collect

When you connect your accounts, we receive training data such as power, heart rate, speed, distance, sleep metrics, HRV, readiness scores, and nutrition information through official APIs. We do not store your login credentials for these services.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to generate personalized training plans (ForgeBlocks™, RacePhase™), fatigue monitoring (Load Index™), recovery recommendations (Recovery Intelligence™), nutrition guidance (FuelSync™), and other AI coaching features. We may also use aggregated, anonymized data to improve our models.

Third-Party Integrations

VeloForge integrates with Garmin, WHOOP, Strava, and TrainingPeaks using their official APIs. Data shared with us is used solely to provide the VeloForge service. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your identifiable data except as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. Integration partners (Garmin, Strava, etc.) have their own privacy policies governing data you share directly with them.

Your Rights

You may disconnect any integrations at any time, request access to or deletion of your data, or opt out of certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at ks@veloforge.app.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.
